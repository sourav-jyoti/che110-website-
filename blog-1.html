TTD launches eco-friendly buses for Tirumala


The Tirumala Tirupati Devasthanams (TTD) has introduced 10 electric buses as a replacement for the present diesel-powered Srivari Dharma Rathas that transport pilgrims to their destinations within Tirumala for free. The change is part of a plan to achieve a net-zero emission target and transform Tirumala into a green town. TTD chairman Y.V. Subba Reddy and its executive officer A.V. Dharma Reddy, launched the 10 eco-friendly buses near the temple in Tirumala on Monday. The chairman thanked M/s Megha Engineering & Infrastructures Ltd (MEIL), Hyderabad, that donated the 10 electric buses to TTD. Each e-bus costs '1.80 crore.
